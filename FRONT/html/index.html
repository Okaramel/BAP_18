Academic Programs, Innovation and Research

Our academic programs develop both soft and hard skills in a trans-disciplinary setting where students collaborate with researchers and PhD students. We use radical learning (experience before theory), anti-disciplinarity (integrated knowledge for creativity), and collective intelligence (community-driven innovation) to promote autonomy and initiative. Inspired by the MIT Media Lab's *Demo or Die*, we explore design at the intersection of AI, cobotics, human-computer interfaces, and biotech. We validate our work through studies, product launches, publications, and partnerships, aiming to create technology that addresses climate action, healthcare, accessibility, and education.
